Assistant Professor

Faculty of Information and Communication Technology · Wrocław University of Science and Technology

I am an Assistant Professor at the Faculty of Information and Communication Technology, Wrocław University of Science and Technology, Poland. My research focuses on Machine Learning, Data Mining, Big Data Analytics, and Complex Networks, with an emphasis on developing scalable algorithms for discovering patterns in large, interconnected, and evolving data. I am also actively involved in teaching courses spanning algorithms, databases, web technologies, and data science.
