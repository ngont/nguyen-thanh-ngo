Notes for Prospective Students

If you are interested in joining my lab as a Ph.D. or M.S. student, please include the following in your email:

  • Your CV / resume
  • Unofficial transcripts
  • A brief statement of your research interests
  • Names of 2–3 references
  • Links to any prior work (papers, code, thesis)

I receive many emails. I am more likely to respond if you demonstrate that you have read at least one of my recent papers and explain how your interests align with my work.
