Teaching

Courses taught at undergraduate and graduate levels

Teaching Philosophy

I believe that effective teaching combines rigorous theoretical foundations with hands-on, project-based learning. My goal is to help students build both a deep conceptual understanding of algorithms and data systems and the practical programming skills needed to apply them. I emphasize learning by building — through programming assignments, projects with real datasets, and live coding — and encourage students to connect course material to current research in machine learning and data mining.
